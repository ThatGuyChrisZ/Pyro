Our Mission Ignited

At We are PYRO, we’re not just watching flames; we’re tracking them with cutting-edge technology. Our drones soar above wildfires, gathering crucial data that transforms chaos into clarity.

Our vision is to create a safer environment by providing real-time insights into wildfire behavior. With our heat map visualizations, we aim to empower firefighters and communities to act swiftly and effectively.

Join us as we blaze a trail in wildfire monitoring, ensuring that every spark is accounted for and every blaze is managed with precision.
